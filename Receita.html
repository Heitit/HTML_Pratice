Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Spaghetti
  • Chopped bacon
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Salt
  • Grated cheese

Instructions

  1. Fry the bacon well until crispy (you can add chopped salami).
  2. Put the pasta to cook in salted water.
  3. In the baking dish where the pasta will be served, beat the eggs well with a fork.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and add grated cheese, also to taste.
  5. When the pasta is ready, drain it and add it (while still hot) to the egg mixture, mixing well.
  6. The heat from the pasta cooks the eggs.
  7. Place the bacon, still warm, over the pasta and "Buon appetito"!
